Why We Are Doing This

The Young Minds Refugee Council gives young refugees the mental health support they need to build a new life in the UK.

1 in 3 young refugees experience severe mental health challenges. Your £1, £1.50, or £2 helps fund real therapy sessions, welcome packs, and peer support groups for young people who need it most.
